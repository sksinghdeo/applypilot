# Safety Model

ApplyPilot uses capability boundaries rather than relying only on warning text.

## Allowed preparation

- extract public job-posting content from the active page;
- compare the posting with user-entered facts;
- route resumes;
- prepare routine contact fields;
- draft reviewable answers;
- keep a local decision log.

## Mandatory review

- work authorization and sponsorship;
- citizenship, permanent residency, export control, and clearance;
- compensation commitments;
- demographic and voluntary self-identification questions;
- background-check authorization;
- legal attestations, electronic signatures, and agreements;
- generated motivation or company-interest answers.

## Disallowed product behavior

- automatic final submission;
- CAPTCHA or 2FA bypass;
- credential harvesting;
- account impersonation;
- fabricated candidate facts;
- unattended mass applications;
- background messaging or connection requests.
