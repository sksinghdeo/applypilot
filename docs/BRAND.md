# ApplyPilot Brand Guide

## Positioning

**Apply thoughtfully. Keep control.**

ApplyPilot is a professional, privacy-first job application copilot—not a mass-application bot.

## Product promise

ApplyPilot reduces repetitive preparation while preserving human control over consequential answers and final submission.

## Logo system

### Full wordmark

Use for repository headers, documentation covers, product landing areas, presentations, and spacious social graphics.

```text
public/assets/applypilot-wordmark.svg
```

### Compact mark

Use for the browser toolbar, favicon, app icon, and square avatars.

```text
public/assets/applypilot-mark.svg
extension/applypilot-mark.svg
extension/icons/icon16.png
extension/icons/icon32.png
extension/icons/icon48.png
extension/icons/icon128.png
```

## Minimum sizes

- Wordmark: 180 px wide
- Compact mark: 16 px
- Social sharing: `media/social-preview.png`

Leave clear space around the mark equal to at least one quarter of its height.

## Voice

ApplyPilot should sound precise, calm, transparent, evidence-based, and user-controlled.

Prefer:

- “The posting appears to require…”
- “Verify this against the original posting.”
- “No hard blocker was detected.”
- “This answer requires review.”

Avoid:

- “Guaranteed match.”
- “You will get the job.”
- “Fully automatic application.”
- “Undetectable automation.”
- “Apply everywhere instantly.”

## Taglines

Primary:

> AI handles the repetition. You keep control.

Positioning:

> A privacy-first, human-in-the-loop job application copilot.

## Repository imagery

Use actual screenshots of the running app with fictional data. Use the prepared social preview for link sharing.

Do not show real personal data, API keys, real resumes, credentials, or functionality not implemented in the app.

## Incorrect usage

Do not stretch, distort, recolor outside the palette, reduce contrast, imply affiliation with a job board, or describe ApplyPilot as an autonomous mass-application bot.
