# ApplyPilot Brand

## Positioning

**Apply thoughtfully. Keep control.**

ApplyPilot is a professional, privacy-first application copilot—not a mass-application bot.

## Logo

The mark combines a geometric **A** with an upward directional arrow. Use the full wordmark for repository and product headers, and the compact mark for the browser toolbar and app icon.

## Colors

- Midnight: `#111936`
- Pilot blue: `#5666F5`
- Electric violet: `#7C4DFF`
- Canvas: `#F2F5FA`
- Success: `#079669`
- Review amber: `#B86B00`

## Voice

- precise and calm;
- user-controlled rather than autonomous;
- transparent about uncertainty;
- never claims to predict hiring decisions.
